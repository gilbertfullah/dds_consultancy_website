What Are Cookies

Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your web browser by a website you visit. A cookie file is stored in your web browser and allows the website or a third-party to recognize you and make your next visit easier and more useful to you.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To understand and save user's preferences for future visits
  • To compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions
  • To enhance and personalize your user experience
  • To help us offer you the most relevant content

Types of Cookies We Use

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Necessary for the website to function properly. These cookies ensure basic functionalities and security features of the website. Session
Functionality Cookies Enable the website to remember choices you make (such as your language or the region you are in) and provide enhanced features. 1 year
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. 2 years
Marketing Cookies Used to track visitors across websites to enable us to display relevant advertisements. 30 days

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the website and deliver advertisements on and through the website.

Please note that third-party services may use cookies that we have no control over.

Your Cookie Choices

You have several choices regarding cookies:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Only accept essential cookies
  • Reject all cookies (may affect site functionality)
  • Modify your specific cookie preferences

How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

Chrome

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Click on Privacy and Security
  3. Select Cookies and other site data

Firefox

  1. Open Menu
  2. Select Options
  3. Choose Privacy & Security

Safari

  1. Go to Preferences
  2. Select Privacy tab
  3. Manage Website Data

Edge

  1. Open Settings
  2. Click on Privacy & services
  3. Manage cookies
