About Development Decision Support (DDS)

Learn about our mission, values, and commitment to excellence in development research.

Empowering Development Excellence through Research and Consulting

Development Decision Support (DDS) is a premier research and consultancy firm that provides actionable insights and strategic guidance to help organizations achieve their development objectives. Specializing in policy analysis, strategic planning, monitoring, and evaluation, we support public, private, and community sectors in designing and implementing impactful development initiatives.

We combine academic rigor with practical experience to ensure that your organization makes informed decisions, maximizes its impact, and drives sustainable development outcomes.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Catalyzing Growth, One Decision at a Time

DDS is driven by a commitment to serve as a catalyst for the success of development initiatives. We offer high-quality research, policy development, and strategic support services, ensuring that our clients make data-driven, effective decisions. We provide tailored solutions that address unique challenges, promote efficient resource allocation, and create long-lasting change.

Our Vision

Shaping the Future of Development

To become the leading center of development research and policy advisory, known for facilitating transformational change and helping organizations realize their development goals. We are committed to fostering innovation and excellence in development practices, building capacity, and supporting the achievement of both local and global development targets.

Our Core Values

What We Offer

DDS provides comprehensive development consulting services tailored to meet the evolving needs of organizations and communities across Africa.

Research & Analysis

  • Detailed economic, financial, and social analyses for projects and programs
  • Custom research for policy formulation and strategic planning

Policy Advisory

  • Expert guidance in creating policies aligned with development goals
  • Well-researched position papers for informed decision-making

Strategic Planning

  • Development of tailored policies and strategies for organizational growth
  • Long-term plans integrating UN 2030 Agenda, SDGs, and AU Agenda 2063

M&E Services

  • Design of robust M&E systems to track project progress
  • Tailored evaluation services for result tracking and impact assessment

Capacity Building

  • Specialized training in project planning and management
  • Workshops designed to strengthen team capacity

Project Design

  • Expert support in the design and formulation of development projects that meet organizational and community needs.
  • Assistance in aligning project goals with broader societal objectives, such as poverty reduction and sustainable development.

Every project is tailored to ensure relevance, sustainability, and measurable success.
